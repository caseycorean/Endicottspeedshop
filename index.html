Weight Distribution Explained

Weight distribution affects how a car accelerates, brakes, and turns. Front-heavy cars tend to understeer, while more balanced setups improve handling and responsiveness.

This calculator helps estimate how modifications like battery relocation, lightweight panels, or engine swaps affect front-to-rear balance.

Even small changes (1–3%) can make a noticeable difference in how a car feels on the road or track.
